1923-05-03 — Assi Rahbani — Assi Rahbani (Arabic: عاصي الرحباني; 4 May 1923 – 21 June 1986) was a Lebanese composer, musician, conductor and producer. He was part of the Rahbani Brothers (Arabic: الأخوان رحباني), with his brother Mansour Rahbani.
1956-01-01 — Ziad Rahbani — Ziad Rahbani (Arabic: زياد الرحباني, romanized:&#160;Ziyād ar-Raḥbānī; 1 January 1956 – 26 July 2025) was a Lebanese composer, pianist, playwright and political commentator. He was the son of Lebanese singer Fairuz and Lebanese composer Assi Rahbani.
